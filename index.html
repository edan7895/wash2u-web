Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a car wash cost in Selangor?

In most parts of Selangor, a basic car wash usually costs around RM15 to RM50. The price mainly depends on the size of the car and whether extra services like waxing or interior cleaning are included.

How long does a normal car wash take?

A regular wash usually takes about 30 to 45 minutes. If you choose full detailing, polishing or coating, the process can take anywhere from 2 to 4 hours depending on the car’s condition.

What’s the difference between polishing and coating?

Polishing focuses on restoring the shine of your car’s paint by removing light scratches and swirl marks. Coating adds a protective layer on top of the paint to help repel dirt, water and UV damage.

How often should I wash my car in Malaysia?

Because of Malaysia’s rain, heat and dust, most people wash their car every 1–2 weeks. Regular washing helps prevent water spots, dirt buildup and paint damage.

Do you offer interior detailing?

Yes. Our interior detailing service includes deep vacuuming, leather or fabric cleaning, dashboard wipe-down and odor removal to keep the cabin fresh.

Is ceramic coating really worth it?

For many car owners, yes. Ceramic coating helps protect the paint from UV rays, rain stains and road dirt while keeping the car glossy for longer.

Should I wash my car during the rainy season?

Yes, actually it can be a good idea. Rainwater often carries dirt and pollutants that stick to your car’s surface. Washing the car helps remove those residues and keeps the paint in better condition.

How much does car polishing cost in Malaysia?

Car polishing in Malaysia usually starts from around RM150 and can go up to RM500 or more depending on the car size and the level of paint correction needed.

Is ceramic coating better than wax?

Wax gives a quick shine but normally lasts only a few weeks. Ceramic coating lasts much longer and offers stronger protection against UV rays, rain and dirt.

Is hand car wash better than automatic wash?

Many car owners prefer hand washing because it is gentler on the paint. Automatic machines are faster, but hand washing usually gives more attention to details.
